远在咫尺

Accompany

A Chinese History and Culture video project.

Directed by Frank Ng

This video told a story about a simple Chinese family. He, a workoholic who believes improving the financial condition is all for a role of father and husband. Frequently breaking promises caused great disappointment and later on quarrels happened. After being frustrated for several times, the wife eventually decided to end their relationship.

This phenonmenon is called widowed marriage, which decribing the behaviour of husband that almost abandoned his family, for reasons like too busy working on his job. We made this video and hope through this the society may aware of the responsibility of a husband is far more than make a living. Accompany is some how even critical, because it always creates a bond between no matter couples, or parents and children.
